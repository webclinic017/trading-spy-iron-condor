## Pillar 1: Capital Preservation (Phil Town Rule #1)

### The Math of Losing

| Loss | Gain Needed to Recover | Time Lost |
| ---- | ---------------------- | --------- |
| -10% | +11%                   | ~6 months |
| -20% | +25%                   | ~1 year   |
| -30% | +43%                   | ~2 years  |
| -50% | +100%                  | ~4 years  |

**One -50% loss erases 4 years of compounding.**

### Preservation Strategies

1. **Position Sizing**: Max 5% risk per trade ($1,500 on $30K)
2. **Stop Losses**: Exit at 200% of credit received
3. **Time Exits**: Close at 7 DTE regardless of P/L
4. **Diversification**: Spread trades across weeks (not same expiration)
5. **Cash Reserve**: Keep 20% in cash for opportunities

### Circuit Breakers

- Halt trading if down 10% in a month
- Review strategy if 3 consecutive losses
- Never revenge trade

### Compounding at Different Rates

### Compounding Killers

1. ❌ Withdrawing profits early
2. ❌ Large losses that require recovery time
3. ❌ Paying unnecessary taxes
4. ❌ Trading fees eating into gains
5. ❌ Emotional decisions interrupting the process

## Pillar 3: Reinvestment Discipline

### The "Don't Touch It" Rule

Every dollar withdrawn is a dollar that can't compound.

### Withdrawal Impact on $30K at 18%

| Scenario                  | Year 7 Balance | Year 10 Balance |
| ------------------------- | -------------- | --------------- |
| 100% reinvest             | $114K          | $157K           |
| Withdraw 25% profits/year | $72K           | $89K            |
| Withdraw 50% profits/year | $51K           | $59K            |

**Withdrawing 50% of profits cuts your Year 10 balance by 62%!**

### Reinvestment Strategy

1. **Phase 1 (Years 1-5)**: 100% reinvestment
2. **Phase 2 (Years 5-7)**: 90% reinvestment, 10% lifestyle
3. **Phase 3 (Year 7+)**: Start taking $6K/month, reinvest surplus
