## Key Insights from Research

### DCA is Your Friend

> "The key to successful investing is not timing the market but time in the market."

Your $25/day deposits will contribute **$9,000/year** regardless of market conditions. Over 6 years, that's **$54,000** in pure deposits alone.

### Credit Spreads > CSPs for Small Accounts

Credit spreads are more capital-efficient:

- CSP on $50 stock needs $5,000 collateral
- Credit spread on SPY needs $500-1,000 collateral
- Similar premium potential, less capital tied up

### Compounding is Exponential

Year 1-3: Slow growth (deposits > returns)
Year 4+: Fast growth (returns > deposits)

This is why consistency matters more than home runs.
