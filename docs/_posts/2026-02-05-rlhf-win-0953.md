The system was generating garbage. So I fixed the system.

## The Problem

Auto-generated blog posts read like robot wrote them

Embarrassing. Publishing bot slop with my name on it.

## What I Tried (And Failed)

templates, mad-libs, formulaic structures

Turns out you can't template your way to authentic content. Who knew?

## The Research

I spent 2 hours researching 2026 SEO best practices:

- **Emotional appeal > dry facts** - People share content that makes them FEEL
- **Story structure** - problem → struggle → pivot → solution → lesson
- **Personal voice** - Show genuine expertise, not corporate speak
- **No templates** - Each story unique, not mad-libs

## The Rewrite

Extract real narrative from context, write actual stories

Now the system:

1. Classifies intent (pivot? prevention? meta?)
2. Extracts structured context
3. Routes to specialized generator
4. Generates actual story with emotional arc

Inspired by Instacart's "brownie recipe problem" - fine-grained context + specialized models.

## The Result

This post you're reading? Auto-generated by the NEW system.

No templates. No bot slop. Actual narrative with emotional arc.

## The Lesson

**Humans share content that makes them FEEL something**

Technology: narrative extraction, 2026 SEO best practices

## Why This Matters

I'm building toward $600K (currently at $101K) by November 2029.

The system that learns is worth more than the system that executes.

**Meta-improvements compound faster than task-level improvements.**
